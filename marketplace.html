Old Clothes Buy Or Rent

Give old campus wear a second life.

Students can sell or rent blazers, shirts, kurta sets, lab coats, and event outfits at much cheaper prices. This makes campus life more affordable and sustainable.

Suggested base prices

Shirt: Rs 250, blazer: Rs 700, lab coat: Rs 300, kurta set: Rs 450, one-day rental from Rs 99.

Useful filters

Size, department, condition, rental or buy, price, seller rating, and delivery in campus.

Why students will like it

Affordable options, less waste, easy access before events, and more value from items already inside campus.
